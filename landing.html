Artist Details


John Smith


Genre: Pop/Rock


Bio: John Smith is a singer-songwriter known for his heartfelt lyrics and dynamic performances. Originally from Nashville, Tennessee, John began his musical journey at a young age, playing in local bands and honing his craft. He gained recognition with his debut album "Heartstrings" and has since toured extensively, captivating audiences with his soulful voice and infectious energy.


Discography:

  1. Album: "Heartstrings" (2018)
  2. Singles: "Hold On", "In My Dreams", "Take Me Home"
